Bachelor Of Information Management
The Bachelor of Information Management (BIM) is a four year eight semester program of the Faculty of Management, Tribhuvan University offering integrated Information Technology and Management courses. This course is envisioned to enable students to develop skills in information technology, and then apply the theories and concepts of a broad, integrated curriculum covering computing, informatics, business fundamentals, and critical and analytical decision-making.
